

## 1. Opinion Essay (Agree / Disagree)

### Introduction

> It is often argued that ________. While some people believe this is true, I completely agree / disagree with this view.

### Body Paragraph 1

> One main reason for my opinion is that ________. This means that ________. For example, ________.

### Body Paragraph 2

> Another important reason is that ________. As a result, ________. A clear example of this is ________.

### Conclusion

> In conclusion, I strongly agree / disagree that ________ because of the reasons mentioned above.

---

## 2. Discussion Essay (Discuss Both Views + Opinion)

### Introduction

> Some people believe that ________, while others argue that ________. This essay will discuss both views before giving my own opinion.

### Body Paragraph 1 (View 1)

> On the one hand, those who support ________ argue that ________. One reason is ________.

### Body Paragraph 2 (View 2)

> On the other hand, people who oppose this view believe that ________. This is because ________.

### Body Paragraph 3 (Your Opinion)

> In my opinion, ________. I believe this because ________.

### Conclusion

> In conclusion, although both views have merits, I believe that ________ is the better approach.

## 3. Advantages / Disadvantages Essay

### Introduction

> Nowadays, ________ has become increasingly common. This essay will discuss the advantages and disadvantages of this trend.

### Body Paragraph 1 (Advantages)

> One major advantage of ________ is that ________. This can lead to ________.

### Body Paragraph 2 (Disadvantages)

> However, there are also some disadvantages. One significant drawback is that ________, which results in ________.

### Conclusion

> In conclusion, although ________ has some disadvantages, its advantages / disadvantages are more significant.

---

## 4. Problem / Solution Essay

### Introduction

> In recent years, ________ has become a serious problem in many countries. This essay will examine the causes of this issue and suggest possible solutions.

### Body Paragraph 1 (Problems / Causes)

> One of the main causes of this problem is ________. As a result, ________.

### Body Paragraph 2 (Solutions)

> To address this issue, governments and individuals can take several measures. One effective solution is ________.

### Conclusion

> In conclusion, although ________ is a serious problem, it can be reduced by implementing appropriate solutions.

---

## 5. Double Question Essay (Two-part Question)

### Introduction

> Recently, ________ has become more common. This essay will explain why this is happening and discuss whether it is a positive or negative development.

### Body Paragraph 1 (Question 1)

> One reason for this trend is ________. This means that ________.

### Body Paragraph 2 (Question 2)

> In my opinion, this development is positive / negative because ________.

### Conclusion

> In conclusion, ________ is increasing due to ________, and overall, it is a positive / negative trend.

---

### ⚠️ Important Exam Tips

* Do **not copy templates word-for-word** too much — change wording slightly
* Always add **specific examples**
* Write **250–290 words**
* Be clear, not complicated
